# MVP Document – Version 1.0 Scope

## Core Promise

The player can create a hockey executive, interview for a first GM job, take over a junior hockey organization, work with an owner, scout/recruit/draft/develop players, upgrade facilities, simulate seasons, track history, and be extended, fired, or offered another job.

## Required for Version 1.0

- Create Executive
- First Job Interview
- Fictional Junior League
- Rulebook-driven league rules
- Owners with trust/confidence/budget/goals
- GM autonomy within owner budget
- Staff hiring/firing
- Players with hidden potential/personality
- Scouting with imperfect reports
- Recruiting with promises
- Annual draft
- Player development
- Basic facilities
- Boxscore game simulation
- History and career stats
- GM reputation and job status
