# Hockey GM Legacy – Project Charter

## One-Sentence Vision

Hockey GM Legacy is a living hockey universe where believable people make understandable decisions, organizations evolve, history is remembered, and every career leaves a lasting legacy.

## Official Tagline

**You are not building a hockey team. You are building hockey lives.**

## Version 1.0 Goal

Start as a junior hockey General Manager, build an organization, make difficult decisions, develop players, work under an owner, and begin a long executive career in a living hockey world.

## Non-Negotiable Design Pillars

1. The world comes first.
2. People matter more than ratings.
3. Information is imperfect.
4. Trust must be earned.
5. Organizations create championships.
6. Stories emerge from systems.
7. History is permanent.
8. Retirement begins a new chapter.
9. Every career should feel unique.
10. The player is building hockey lives, not just hockey teams.
11. The world should remember.
12. Every major decision must be explainable.
13. Nothing in the simulation should assume one league.

## First Implementation Anchor

The first implementation skeleton should be the **Rule Engine v1**, because every hockey action depends on whether the league rulebook allows it.
