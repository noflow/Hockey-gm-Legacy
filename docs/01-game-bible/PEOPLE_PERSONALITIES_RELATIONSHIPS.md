# People, Personalities, and Relationships

Every human in the game is a Person.

A Person may become:

- Player
- Coach
- Scout
- GM
- Owner
- Agent
- Doctor
- Parent
- Media member

Every person has:

- Identity
- Personality
- Goals
- Trust
- Memories
- Relationships
- Reputation
- Career history

Relationships are directional and may include trust, respect, confidence, loyalty, influence, friendship, and rivalry.
