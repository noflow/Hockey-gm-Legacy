# Talent, Development, and Potential

Talent is uncertain.

Potential is not a promise.

A player develops through:

- Natural talent
- Work ethic
- Coachability
- Ice time
- Facilities
- Coaching quality
- Confidence
- Health
- Organizational stability
- Luck

The game must support:

- Generational talent
- Franchise players
- Late bloomers
- Busts
- Hidden gems
- Draft steals
- Injury what-if careers
