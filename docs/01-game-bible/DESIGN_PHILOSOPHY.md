# Design Philosophy

Hockey GM Legacy is a career-based hockey universe simulator.

The player manages a lifelong executive career, not a single franchise forever.

## Pillars

- The world comes first.
- Every career is unique.
- Certainty is impossible.
- Relationships matter.
- Stories matter more than statistics.
- History is permanent.

## Core Experience

The player begins as an unknown executive, likely in junior hockey, and builds a career through decisions, relationships, scouting, trust, reputation, and organizational success.
