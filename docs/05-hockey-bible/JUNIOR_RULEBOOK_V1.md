# Junior Rulebook v1

## League Structure

- 10–16 teams
- Regular season
- Playoffs
- Championship
- Annual draft
- Recruiting
- Owner budgets

## Suggested Defaults

- Minimum roster: 18
- Maximum roster: 25
- Active roster: 20
- Goalies required: 2
- Overage slots: 3
- Import slots: 2
- Injured reserve: enabled
- Reserve list: enabled

## Recruiting Factors

- Ice time
- Development
- Education
- Facilities
- Winning
- Distance from home
- Parents
- Billet quality
- Promises
- Team reputation
