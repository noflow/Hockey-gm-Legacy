# Contract Framework v1

## Version 1 Required Contract Types

- Junior player agreement
- Staff contract
- GM contract
- Scout contract
- Coach contract

## Junior Player Agreements

May include:

- Term
- Stipend or salary
- Education package
- Housing support
- Billet arrangement
- Playing role promise
- Development promise
- Release/transfer conditions

## Future Professional Contract Support

Future rulebooks should support:

- Annual salary
- Term
- Signing bonus
- Performance bonuses
- Two-way salary
- No-trade clauses
- No-move clauses
- Team/player options
- Buyouts
- Retained salary
- Arbitration
- RFA/UFA
- Qualifying offers
- Offer sheets
- Entry-level contracts
- Veteran minimums

## Autonomy Rule

The GM does not need owner approval for normal contracts within approved budget and league rules.

The owner judges outcomes later.
