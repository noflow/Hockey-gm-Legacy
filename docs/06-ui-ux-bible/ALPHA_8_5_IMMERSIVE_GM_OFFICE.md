# Alpha 8.5 Immersive GM Office Experience

## Goal

Alpha 8.5 turns the first AlphaDesktop screen into a front-office morning briefing. The goal is immersion, workflow, and presentation only. No gameplay systems, AI engines, simulation logic, Godot work, voice, music, or animation engine were added.

## GM Office Home

The Dashboard content now opens as a GM Office. It leads with:

- Good Morning greeting using the GM first name.
- Current weekday and calendar date.
- Next game and last game context.
- Owner mood, confidence, urgent, important, and routine badges.
- Assistant GM quote or recommended action.
- Top media headline.
- Quick actions for advancing, reviewing the Action Center, opening Inbox, and saving.

## Morning Briefing Cards

The office uses card-based sections instead of a metric wall:

- Today's Agenda
- Assistant GM
- Coach Morning Report
- Head Scout Report
- Medical Report
- Owner Report
- Recent Transactions

Each card gives a short morning note and a direct action into the relevant workspace.

## Contextual Sidebar

The right side of the office acts as a contextual sidebar:

- Organization Snapshot
- League Snapshot
- Player of Interest

The Player of Interest card can highlight an injured player, prospect, scouting target, or roster player, and links to the person card and dossier.

## Navigation Feel

Cards now connect the home screen to existing workspaces rather than acting as static text. The pass relies on existing Alpha 8.4 navigation history, breadcrumbs, focus handling, and feedback states.

## Visual Hierarchy

Important items are shown as large cards. Routine context is shown as smaller side cards and badges. The office avoids the older dense dashboard metric wall and keeps the first screen focused on what the GM should review this morning.

## No New Gameplay

Alpha 8.5 is UI and presentation only. It does not add or change game simulation, contract logic, trade logic, draft logic, AI behavior, or engine-side hockey rules.
