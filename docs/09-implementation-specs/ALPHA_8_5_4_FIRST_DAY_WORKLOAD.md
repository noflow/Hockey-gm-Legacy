# Alpha 8.5.4 - New GM Inheritance & First-Day Workload

## Purpose

A new GM begins with a functioning organization, not an empty sandbox or an overwhelming task list.

## Inherited State

The scenario retains the existing roster, contracts, staff, line combinations, development plans, scouting work, prospect rights, prior history, budget context, and market state. The onboarding layer does not delete or resolve any of those records.

## Day 1

The Action Center shows three curated items:

1. The highest-priority open contract-rights decision when one exists.
2. The owner mandate.
3. An inherited roster recommendation.

The initial inbox contains the owner welcome, head coach roster assessment, head scout summary, Assistant GM briefing, and a medical note only when it is meaningful.

## First-Week Rollout

The Action Center uses a small staged limit while normal work remains visible in its own workspace:

- Day 1: 3 actions.
- Day 3: up to 5 actions.
- Day 5: up to 7 actions.
- Day 7 onward: normal Action Center behavior.

Only open items are displayed, duplicate IDs are removed, and the opening contract, owner, and roster actions are chosen first.

## Assistant GM Briefing

The briefing states the team identity and competitive window, three strengths, three concerns, the key contract decision, best prospect, biggest roster need, and a recommended first action. It appears in the starting inbox and dashboard context.

## Boundaries

This is a presentation and onboarding layer. It adds no new gameplay simulation, AI decision rules, Godot dependency, or persistence system.
