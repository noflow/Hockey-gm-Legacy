# Player Knowledge Model

## Core Principle

The purpose of scouting is not to discover the truth.

The purpose of scouting is to reduce uncertainty.

## Constitution Update

Certainty is impossible. The General Manager's responsibility is to make the best decisions possible with imperfect information.

## Knowledge Categories

### Facts
Name, age, position, height, team, statistics.

### Observations
Things someone saw directly.

### Opinions
Human judgments, projections, concerns, recommendations.

### Unknowns
True ceiling, long-term development, future injuries, pressure handling, eventual legacy.

## Player Dossier

Players are shown through dossiers, not rating pages.

Sections:

- Executive Summary
- Facts
- Scout Opinions
- Analytics Report
- Medical Report
- Character Report
- Relationships
- Career Timeline
- History
- GM Notebook

## Design Rule

The player should rarely see exact ratings.

The GM is deciding who to believe.
