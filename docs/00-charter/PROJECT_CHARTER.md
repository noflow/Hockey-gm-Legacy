# Project Charter

## Vision

Build a living hockey universe where believable people make understandable decisions, organizations evolve, history is remembered, and every career leaves a legacy.

## Tagline

**You are not building a hockey team. You are building hockey lives.**

## Design North Stars

- The player is not choosing between ratings. The player is choosing between opinions.
- Certainty is impossible. The GM's responsibility is to make the best decisions possible with imperfect information.
- The world should remember.

## Version 1.0 Goal

Start as a junior hockey General Manager, build an organization, work under an owner, make difficult decisions, develop players, and begin a long executive career in a living hockey world.

## Architecture Pillars

1. Legacy Engine
2. Event Engine
3. Human Intelligence System
4. Communication Engine
5. Rule Engine

## Non-Negotiable Principles

1. The world comes first.
2. People matter more than ratings.
3. Certainty is impossible.
4. Trust must be earned.
5. Organizations create championships.
6. Stories emerge from systems.
7. History is permanent.
8. Retirement begins a new chapter.
9. Every career should feel unique.
10. The player is building hockey lives, not simply hockey teams.
11. The world should remember.
12. Every major decision must be explainable.
13. Nothing in the simulation should assume one league.
14. Knowledge is earned.
