# Hockey GM Legacy Manifesto

We are not making a hockey game.

We are building a living hockey universe.

We are not building menus.

We are building careers.

We are not building ratings.

We are building opinions.

We are not building AI.

We are building believable people.

We are not building statistics.

We are building memories.

We are not building seasons.

We are building history.

We are not building teams.

We are building organizations.

We are not building championships.

We are building legacies.

## Anti-Manifesto

We will not expose information the GM should not know.

We will not let the AI cheat.

We will not create drama through scripting when systems can create it naturally.

We will not add numbers when language tells the story better.

We will not remove history.

We will not let a feature exist in isolation.

We will not sacrifice long-term careers for short-term convenience.

We will not make the player ask, "What is his Overall?"

We will make the player ask, "Who do I believe?"
