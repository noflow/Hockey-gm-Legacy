# Hockey GM Legacy
# Master Development Roadmap v2

## Development Philosophy

Every milestone represents one complete system. A milestone is not complete until it has been reviewed across Game Bible, Technical Bible, AI Bible, Communication Systems, Hockey Bible, and UI / UX Bible.

## Phase 1 – Foundation

### Milestone 001 — Foundation ✅
Completed: vision, constitution, Legacy Engine, HIS, Event Engine, Communication Engine, Living World, people-first architecture, and history philosophy.

### Milestone 002 — Rule Engine
Make every league operate from configurable rulebooks instead of hardcoded hockey rules.

Required: rulebook data structure, league rule loading, roster rules, contract rules, draft rules, playoff rules, eligibility rules, budget rules, and modding-ready JSON structure.

### Milestone 003 — Owners
Required: owner personality, goals, trust/confidence, budgets, autonomy framework, meetings, GM evaluation, extensions/firings.

### Milestone 004 — Scouting
Required: scout roles, regions, reports, confidence, hidden potential, GM personal scouting, draft board integration.

### Milestone 005 — Recruiting
Required: recruit priorities, parents, promises, commitments, interest levels, team reputation.

### Milestone 006 — Coaches & Staff
Required: coach roles, scout roles, medical staff, staff hiring/firing, staff contracts, staff relationships.

## Phase 2 – Player Systems
Milestones 007–011: Player Development, Injuries & Medical, Contracts, Trades, Draft.

## Phase 3 – Organization
Milestones 012–015: Facilities, Organization Culture, Finance, Staff Relationships.

## Phase 4 – Hockey
Milestones 016–019: League Rules, Game Simulation, Season Flow, International Hockey.

## Phase 5 – World
Milestones 020–023: Media, Fans, History, Hockey Reference.

## Phase 6 – Career
Milestones 024–026: GM Career, Career Progression, Retirement.

## Phase 7 – User Experience
Milestones 027–031: GM Office, War Room, Organization Headquarters, Player Profiles, UI Polish.

## Phase 8 – Technology
Milestones 032–035: Save System, Mod Support, Performance, Testing.

## MVP Guardrail

Version 1.0 only implements what is needed to make the junior GM career loop fun and replayable.
