# Hockey Bible
## Junior Rulebook v1

## League Structure

- 10–16 teams
- Regular season
- Playoffs
- Championship
- Annual draft
- Recruiting
- Owner budgets

## Roster Rules

Suggested default:

- Minimum roster: 18
- Maximum roster: 25
- Active roster: 20
- Goalies required: 2
- Overage slots: 3
- Import slots: 2
- Injured reserve: enabled
- Reserve list: enabled

## Eligibility

- Minimum age: 15
- Maximum age: 20
- Overage status begins at league-configured age
- Draft eligibility controlled by rulebook
- Recruiting eligibility controlled by rulebook

## Recruiting Factors

Ice time, development, education, facilities, winning, distance from home, parents, billet quality, promises, and team reputation.

## Draft Rules

Annual draft, reverse standings draft order, configurable rounds, eligible player pool, draft history recorded.

## Budget Rules

Junior teams operate under owner budgets rather than hard salary caps.
